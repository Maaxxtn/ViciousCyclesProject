About Vicious Cycles

At Vicious Cycles we value community and experiences. We want to share the world of biking with you, whoever you are, so we established to be a different kind of bike shop. We strive to be a bike shop that provides a sense of community. We don't want to be just a place to shop at, but a place to trade, learn, make friendships, and hang out. Inspired to create events and community around cycling and bring back being in the present moment of a fast paced world that views growth as sales and not experiences.

Hang around and be sure to check out our extensive inventory online and inside the shop! We also offer maintenance services and provide easy pick up times and scheduling. Or, just stop by and have lunch or a coffee before, after, or, just a little break, in the middle of a lengthy ride.
